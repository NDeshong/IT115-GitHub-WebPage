Social Media

Social media is a network that connects its users to share information, contents, and knowledge with each other. It gives the users quick electronic communication of contents such as news, updates, images, articles, and videos. Nowadays, one of the greatest ways to share, exchange, and communicate information with other people anywhere in the world is through social media. Popular platforms we see today are Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and many more. Looking into the future, it is guaranteed that social media will keep growing and evolving along with its demand, as users and influencers will increase, and companies will rely more on it to expand on their products, sales, and customers.

Getting Started

In order to begin working in social media, consider becoming a social media developer. Social media developers focus on creating and implementing methods that engage an audience. Additionally, they ensure that applications function properly through testing and maintenance phases. Choosing the right major is the first step towards getting started. Many employers are more likely to hire you if you have a Bachelor's degree or Associate's in Computer Science, Web Design, or another similar field. After college, it is critical to pursue further education such as gaining internships to enhance your experience and getting certified in digital design through online training courses.

Required Skills

  • Analytical skills to be able to create applications and interpret client's needs
  • Experience with Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite, & Video Production
  • Programming Languages-Javascript, HTML/CSS, Python, etc
  • Excellent Communication skills to collaborate with co-workers & discus designs with clients
  • Determination to be able to meet deadlines when working on multiple projects
